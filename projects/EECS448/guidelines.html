Homework Submission Guidelines

 

1. For Typed and Printed Homework Assignments


When you submit your printed homework, please follow the guidelines listed below.

  • Your full official name and student ID should appear at the top left corner.
  • More than one pages must be stapled in the top left corner.
    • Example:



      John Smith
      2799999
      EECS 448

      Homework #3

      Prompt : Describe the waterfall model of software developm...

        . . . .

     

2. For Handwritten Homework Assignments


When you submit your handwritten homework, please follow the guidelines listed below.

  • Full official name and your KU ID Number should appear in the top right corner.
  • More than one pages must be stapled in the top left corner.
  • Handwritten submissions must be legible.

Please turn in your homework in class on time!
