Scanning Passport

 

From your passport, scan the page with your photograph and birthdate.

 

  1. Place your Passport in the top-right corner on the scanner glass and close the lid.
  2. Click on the scanner icon on your Desktop.
  3. Click on the PDF icon as shown in the picture below.
  4. Check the file on the Desktop. It must be clear and readable.

 

  Scanning Visa

 

Scan your Valid or Most Recent Visa.

 

  1. Open your passport to the Visa page.
  2. Place it in the top-right corner on the scanner glass and close the lid.
  3. Click on the scanner icon on your Desktop.
  4. Click on the PDF icon as before.
  5. Check the file on the Desktop. It must be clear and readable.
